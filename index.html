You are invited!!


We cordially invite you to our wedding ceremony and reception on Saturday, December 25th, two thousand and twenty-three, at six o'clock in the evening, at the Grand Hotel, 123 Main Street, New York. A black tie outfit is required. We look forward to seeing you on this special day.
